The 1982 Vertical Masterclass

Experience Description

Enter the hallowed grounds of a private 18th-century cellar in the heart of Bordeaux for an unprecedented vertical tasting of the legendary 1982 vintage. Often cited as the year that changed the wine world forever, the 1982 vintage represents the pinnacle of balance and longevity. Under the guidance of Master Sommelier Marc-Antoine J., guests will explore five distinct First Growth estates, decanted with meticulous care to reveal the evolutionary journey of these liquid masterpieces over four decades.

Host Biography: Marc-Antoine J.

With over 30 years of experience in the vineyards of Saint-Émilion and Pauillac, Marc-Antoine is one of only a handful of professionals to have curated private collections for three royal families. His expertise lies in the "quiet life" of the bottle—understanding exactly when a vintage has reached its spiritual peak.

Frequently Asked

Can I request specific vintages for a private tasting? expand_more

Yes. Our concierge team can work with our network of private collectors and estates to source specific vintages not currently listed in our standard experiences, subject to availability and market pricing.

Are these experiences available for non-members? expand_more

Select experiences are available for single-use booking, though OPV Members receive priority scheduling, preferred pricing, and access to "Vault-Only" vertical tastings.

Is transportation included? expand_more

For all "Elite" tier experiences, chauffeur service in a late-model luxury vehicle is provided from your residence or hotel to the tasting location and back.
