Frequently Asked Questions

HOW ARE PRIVATE EVENTS CURATED? expand_more
Our curation team monitors high-value social calendars across five continents. Private invitations are extended based on your membership tier, professional profile, and previous experience history within the OPV network.
WHAT IS THE REVIEW PROCESS FOR INVITATION REQUESTS? expand_more
Once a request is submitted, our concierge team reviews the applicant's credentials and availability. For high-security events, a background check may be required. You will be notified of the outcome within 48 hours.
CAN I HOST MY OWN EVENT THROUGH OPV? expand_more
Yes, OPV Members can leverage our planning team to host private gatherings. Every proposed event must pass our "Verified by OPV" audit to ensure it meets our network's expectations of quality and privacy.
