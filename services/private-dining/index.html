Can the menu be fully customized? expand_more
Absolutely. Once a booking request is initiated, our culinary concierge will facilitate a direct session with the chef to tailor every course to your specific palate and dietary requirements.

For our ultra-exclusive portfolios, we recommend a 3-week lead time. However, for active members, the OPV Concierge can often secure slots with as little as 48 hours notice depending on chef availability.
